Ingredients:
- 1 lb. ground meat (beef, turkey)
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- canned diced tomatoes, 14.5 oz
- tomato sauce, 8 oz.
- tabasco sauce
- worcestershire sauce
- can of beer
- salt and pepper
  
Instructions:
- season ground meat, sautee until brown
- add onion and garlic to meat mixture, sautee til translucent
- add tabasco sauce and worcestershire sauce, sautee for a minute more
- then add beer, cook til liquid evaporated by half
- add diced tomatoes and tomato sauce, cook until liquid has mostly evaporated
- season to taste
